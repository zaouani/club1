AKTIION-2024

Shaping the Future of Innovation

The AIAC Industrial Club is proud to have been a key organizer of the AKTIION Conference (Advanced Knowledge, Techniques, and Decisions for Development Challenges), held from November 19 to 21, 2024. This prestigious event brought together experts and participants to exchange insights on critical topics such as Energy and Environment, Healthcare Systems Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Emerging Technologies. Each day featured an engaging mix of keynote speeches, parallel sessions, and doctoral abstract presentations, offering a platform for discussing innovative solutions to global challenges.

The AKTIION Conference 2024 brought together leading experts to share insights on critical topics, including sustainable development by Pr. Audrey Loridan-Baudrier, green hydrogen by Youssef Naimi, human-machine interfaces by Mohamed Tayeb Laskri, and Morocco's energy transition by Amin Bennouna. The event also featured Maria Di Mascolo on healthcare system engineering and concluded with a focus on Industrial Engineering, coordinated by Pr. Lina Aboueljinane and Dr. Abdourahim Sylla, providing a comprehensive exploration of innovative solutions and industry advancements.

Parallel sessions throughout the event showcased cutting-edge research through the presentation of doctoral abstracts. These sessions covered a wide range of topics, including energy, technology, the environment, and industry. This structure allowed participants to gain deeper insights into current advancements and fostered dialogue between researchers, professionals, and academics.

The conference also emphasized networking and cultural exchange. The gala dinner offered an opportunity for meaningful discussions with members and executives, while the final day concluded with an Awards Ceremony, partnership signings, and a Casablanca city tour. These elements, combined with the rich content of the sessions, made the event both professionally enriching and culturally inspiring. Special thanks are extended to Mr. Mohamed Rafi, whose dedication ensured the conference's success.
